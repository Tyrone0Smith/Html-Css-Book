Image Alignment Horizontally

  • left
  • center
Images are a great way to showcase your work and make your website more visually appealing.

You can use the "align" attribute to control the alignment of images on your page.
For example, you can use

<img src="image.jpg" align="left">

to align an image to the left.

Image Alignment Vertically

  • top
  • middle
  • bottom

You can use the "valign" attribute to control the vertical alignment of images on your page.
For example, you can use

<img src="image.jpg" valign="top">

to align an image to the top.
